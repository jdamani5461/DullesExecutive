Disqualifying Offenses:

Per DBHDS regulations, applicants are disqualified if convicted of:

  • Any felony involving abuse, neglect, or exploitation
  • Sexual offenses or crimes against minors
  • Violent crimes within the past 5 years
  • Fraud, embezzlement, or crimes of dishonesty
  • Drug distribution or manufacturing

* Some convictions may be considered on a case-by-case basis depending on the nature of the offense, time elapsed, and rehabilitation evidence.
