Direct Support Professionals

Who They Are:

Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) are the backbone of group home care. They assist residents with daily living activities, medication administration (under supervision), community integration, and behavioral support.

What We Require:

  • CPR & First Aid certification
  • DBHDS-approved training (within 30 days of hire)
  • Background check clearance (CPS, criminal, FBI)
  • 1+ years of experience in ID/DD settings (preferred)

Availability:

Full-time, part-time, per diem, overnight shifts. Emergency placements available.

Medication Technicians

Who They Are:

Certified Medication Technicians (Med Techs) are trained to safely administer medications, document dosages, and monitor resident health under nursing supervision.

What We Require:

  • Virginia Medication Technician certification
  • CPR & First Aid current
  • DBHDS training compliance
  • 2+ years in group home or assisted living settings

Availability:

Day, evening, and overnight shifts. Per diem and permanent placements.

Program Managers / House Managers

Who They Are:

Experienced leaders who oversee daily operations, staff supervision, compliance documentation, resident care plans, and coordination with case managers and families.

What We Require:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in human services
  • 3+ years supervisory experience in ID/DD
  • Knowledge of Virginia licensing regulations
  • Strong communication and crisis management skills

Availability:

Full-time, salaried positions.
