Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How quickly can you fill an urgent need?

A: For emergency requests, we typically place qualified staff within 4-12 hours. Standard placements take 24-48 hours. We maintain a pool of vetted per diem caregivers specifically for urgent needs.

Q: What are your fees?

A: Our fees vary based on position type, urgency, and duration. We offer competitive rates with no hidden costs. Contact us for a customized quote. First-time clients receive a 10% discount on their first placement.

Q: Do you require long-term contracts?

A: No. We work on a per-placement basis with no long-term contracts required. Many clients choose to establish ongoing partnerships, but you're never locked in.

Q: What if the caregiver doesn't work out?

A: We offer a 90-day guarantee. If there are performance issues or the fit isn't right, we replace the caregiver at no additional charge. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Q: How do you ensure DBHDS compliance?

A: Every caregiver completes DBHDS-approved training, background checks, and credential verification before placement. We maintain digital files accessible for inspections and provide automated compliance tracking.

Q: Can we hire your staff permanently?

A: Yes! We offer temp-to-perm placements. If you'd like to hire one of our caregivers full-time after a trial period, we have a conversion fee structure that makes the transition smooth.
